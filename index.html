Our Mission

We are dedicated to providing accessible, inclusive, and high-quality education to empower individuals, strengthen communities, and build a brighter future for all.

Who We Are

Olive Educational Development Center (OEDC) and Olive Community College (OCC) are committed to breaking down barriers to education. We provide digital literacy, vocational training, and academic programs for migrant workers, refugees, and displaced youth in Mae Sot and surrounding border areas.

What We Do

Our programs are designed to meet the unique needs of our community, providing practical skills and knowledge for self-reliance and growth.

Digital Literacy

Training for youth and teachers in essential computer skills, online safety, and modern teaching methodologies.

Vocational Training

Courses in ICT, language, and community leadership to prepare students for meaningful employment.

Academic Development

GED preparation and curriculum design tailored for migrant and refugee students seeking higher education.
