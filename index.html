About Us

Our Story

Since our establishment, Olive Education has been dedicated to transforming lives through education on the Thai-Myanmar border. We began as a small community initiative and have grown into a vibrant educational center, serving thousands of migrant and displaced youth.

Our journey reflects the resilience of our community and our commitment to creating lasting positive change through education, digital literacy, and vocational training.

Olive Community

Our Mission

  • Provide inclusive and safe education tailored for marginalized migrant and displaced communities.
  • Equip learners with digital literacy skills for modern challenges.
  • Deliver vocational training, including ICT, language, agriculture, and leadership.
  • Support academic development and pathways for higher education.
  • Foster community empowerment through advocacy and rights awareness.
  • Ensure sustainable, culturally sensitive programs that respect local contexts.

Our Vision

  • Empower communities to achieve self-reliance through education and skill development.
  • Create accessible opportunities for all migrants and displaced persons to thrive.
  • Build resilient, informed, and digitally capable societies.
  • Promote social justice, inclusion, and advocacy as core pillars of growth.
  • Develop a network of strong educational institutions for positive change.

Who We Are

Olive Educational Development Center (OEDC) and Olive Community College (OCC) are passionate about breaking down barriers to education. We empower migrant workers, refugees, and displaced youth in Mae Sot and border areas with digital skills, vocational training, and academic support.

Our dedicated team of educators, innovators, and community leaders works tirelessly to create meaningful educational opportunities that transform lives and build stronger communities.

Our Programs

Transforming lives through education and empowerment on both sides of the Thai-Myanmar border

Overview

OEDC serves the migrant and displaced communities in Mae Sot, Thailand, providing essential education and skills training. Our center focuses on creating a safe, inclusive learning environment that bridges educational gaps and promotes digital literacy.

Key Activities
  • Digital skills training for youth and teachers
  • Teacher professional development programs
  • Language courses (Thai, English, Burmese)
  • Community education initiatives
Impact
  • 3,000+ students trained in digital literacy
  • 150+ teachers certified in modern teaching methods
  • 500+ language program graduates
  • 20+ community partnerships established

Overview

OCC operates within Myanmar's border regions, delivering comprehensive education and vocational training programs. We focus on building sustainable skills and fostering community leadership through practical, hands-on learning experiences.

Key Activities
  • Vocational training programs
  • Youth leadership development
  • Community advocacy training
  • Media and communications workshops
Impact
  • 2,000+ vocational program graduates
  • 100+ community leaders trained
  • 50+ successful community projects
  • 15+ media initiatives launched
